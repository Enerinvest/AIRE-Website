Team enablement

Same AI, same firm, same output — every time.

Ask two people to prompt an AI for an investment memo and you'll get two different documents — different structure, different tone, different level of detail. DealCase.ai standardizes both halves of that problem: what your team asks, and what comes back.

The result isn't just faster output — it's output your IC, your compliance team, and your clients can trust looks the same no matter who produced it.

A shared prompt library

Every case starts from built-in, editable prompts for what your team actually does — an asset summary, a DD pass, a partner briefing — one click to run, refined once and reused by everyone, instead of reinvented per person, per week.

Templates that enforce your house format

Upload your firm's actual memo, financial model, deck, or agreement template with tagged placeholders — DealCase.ai fills the data automatically and drafts the AI-authored sections in place. Same structure, same sections, same look, whether it's your newest analyst or your most senior one running it.

The product

Everything about a property, in the conversation about it.

A structured relational core sits behind every chat — the difference between "upload a file and ask about it" and a system that actually knows your portfolio. Every chat, asset and report is shared across the team by default, so progress is trackable and every output follows the same house standard, not whoever drafted it that week.

Portfolio

Every asset, with the fields that matter to your firm.

A central register of every property you hold or track, with up to ~100 structured fields — identity, location, physical condition, ownership, financials, and lease terms. Start from a standard template or define your own; bulk import and export via Excel keeps it in sync with how your team already works.

Why AI, why now

Real estate runs on documents. AI was built to read them.

The industry's expensive manual work — reading data rooms, producing memos, tracking deadlines — is exactly the work that's automatable, without removing human judgement from the loop.

Grounded, not generic

The AI answers from your firm's actual assets, contacts and documents — not general web knowledge.

Documents are the data

Leases, scans, spreadsheets, even property photos — text extraction and vision mean the AI reads what you already have.

Your firm's own vocabulary

A cap-rate fund and a rental-yield fund shouldn't get the same answer — per-tenant AI context makes it match how you talk.

Narrative + data, automated

Memos and teasers are data plus narrative — the template engine automates the data, AI drafts the narrative, a human reviews.

Always current, on demand

A chat over live data means the "report" is generated fresh each time — never a PDF that was accurate the day it was exported.

Infrastructure you don't have to build

Isolation, per-tenant context, and secure integrations are hard to build well — DealCase.ai gives you the benefit without the build.
