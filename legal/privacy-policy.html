We only collect information you choose to give us. Currently, that's limited to the "Open Account" form on this site, which asks for:

We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How long we keep it

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and, if you become a customer, for the duration of that relationship plus a reasonable period afterward for legitimate business and legal record-keeping purposes. If you'd like your information deleted sooner, contact us using the details below and we'll action it.
